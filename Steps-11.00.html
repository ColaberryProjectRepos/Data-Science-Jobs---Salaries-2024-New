Importing Data into Power BI 11

This Dataset can be accessed by clicking the Red Link in the top left corner.  




Dataset: Glassdoor_Salary


Dataset Glassdoor_Jobs



Original Data source link:
Dataset Link: Data Science Jobs & Salaries 2024 (kaggle.com)

1.    First you will need to have the data saved on your device.  
2.    Open Power BI Desktop.
3.    Click on Get data ribbon
4.    Select Excel Workbook and then click connect
5.    Open "glassdoor_jobs" and "Glassdoor_Salary_Cleaned" excel files
6.    Select the table, a preview of data will be displayed, click on Load.
