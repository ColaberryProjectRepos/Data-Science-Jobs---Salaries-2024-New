Lessons Learned:
Project Enhancements and Solutions

Data Quality Management:
Initial challenges related to data inconsistencies and incompleteness were addressed through a rigorous data cleansing and validation process, ensuring reliability and analytical integrity.

Data Acquisition & Transformation:
The original dataset lacked sufficient depth for comprehensive analysis. This limitation was resolved by augmenting the dataset and applying robust transformation techniques to enrich the data structure.

Model Optimization:
Several DAX formulas required refinement to enhance accuracy. This was accomplished through iterative testing and continuous adjustment, resulting in reliable and validated outputs.

Visualization Effectiveness:
Clear communication of insights was achieved by refining data visualizations. Enhancements to visual design improved interpretability and ensured that findings were easily understood by stakeholders.
