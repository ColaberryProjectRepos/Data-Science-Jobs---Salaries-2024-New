Deployment 33

Here in this Step of Deployment. In order to Deploy you will have to go to your Microsoft Fabric. Open up your Dashboard that you have been working on. Click on File, then go down to Embed Report. Then go down to Publish to Web (Public). 

The next pop up will give you the Link for your Dashboard. You will Also have the Embed Code as well.

Now your Project is Deployed and can be shared with anyone.
